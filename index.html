I love turning ideas into something real through design. With a GPA of 3.8/4.0 at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, my journey combines usability testing, A/B experiments, and design thinking to build products that are both beautiful and genuinely usable.

I focus on creating user interfaces that serve a real purpose – making sure they're not just pretty, but actually solve problems. From digitizing manual university systems to running usability research with 152 participants, my goal is to make something that feels natural and easy to use.

I'm a bit of a perfectionist when it comes to the small stuff, but I think that's what makes good design great. This attention to detail helps me build strong relationships with users, as they know I'll put the same care into inclusive, accessible experiences.
